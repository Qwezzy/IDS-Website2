Our Philosophy

At the core of our philosophy lies the concept of 'strategic fit.' We recognize that technology's value is maximized when closely aligned with business drivers and processes. Our commitment is to offer holistic, integrated, and business-centric information technology solutions that directly tackle identified business challenges.

This commitment is reflected in our business policies, where we continuously seek to attract top-notch talent, employ leading information technology practices, and uphold broader socio-economic principles of representation and empowerment. With a team comprising 10 permanent staff members and hosting 2 IT interns, we strive for excellence in all aspects of our operations.

We Deliver Results

Our Mission

As providers of I.C.T. strategies and services, we are dedicated to delivering enduring commercial benefits aligned with our clients' fundamental business requirements. Our strategies are designed to be economical, efficient, durable, and flexible, enabling organizations to respond promptly to market and customer needs.

Our Story

Established in 2009 by a dynamic group of young professionals, Intermediate Data Systems aims to deliver exceptional professional services in the Information Technology space. Our team members bring diverse expertise in the ICT field, boasting strong entrepreneurial backgrounds and a proven track record of success.

Our Vision

Our vision is rooted in enhancing community welfare and development through a combination of public-private partnerships and internal initiatives. We are committed to ensuring customer satisfaction by fostering continuous collaboration, promoting visionary thinking, unleashing creativity, and crafting high-standard solutions while maximizing the return on IT investments.
